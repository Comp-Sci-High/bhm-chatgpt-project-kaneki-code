Sleepy Hallow
An Artist,
Influencer

Sleepy Hallow started making music as a teenager, initially freestyling with friends and recording songs in makeshift home studios. He was inspired by the growing Brooklyn drill scene, which was pioneered by artists like Pop Smoke and 22Gz.

Bit About Sleepy

Sleepy Hallow, whose real name is Tegan Price, is a rising star in the Brooklyn drill music scene. Known for his laid-back yet menacing flow, melodic hooks, and gritty storytelling, he has become one of the most prominent figures in the genre. Sleepy Hallow was influenced by the sounds of dancehall, reggae, and hip-hop, which are deeply rooted in his Jamaican heritage. These influences are evident in his music, which often blends Caribbean rhythms with Brooklyn drill beats.
